<objective>
Wrap createPayment + settlePayment + updatePayment in an outer db.transaction() so a failed settlement doesn't leave orphaned payment rows that cause duplicates on retry.
</objective>

<context>
See [15-CONTEXT.md](./15-CONTEXT.md) and [15-PATTERNS.md](./15-PATTERNS.md) for domain remediation background.

**Current bug**: `services/api-ts/src/handlers/association:member/recordDuesPayment.ts` lines 44-67:
1. Creates payment row (persisted immediately)
2. Calls `settlePayment()` (allocates funds, extends expiry)
3. Updates payment status to 'completed'

If step 2 fails, the payment row persists with no allocations or expiry extension. Retrying creates a duplicate payment row. `settlePayment()` already uses an inner transaction for its own operations, but the outer create→settle→update sequence is not atomic.
</context>
